Live drum tracks and custom loops

Hello! My name is Terran Taylor. I'm drummer/percussionist located in the metro Atlanta area. I will record live drums for songs in my recording studio space. Files will be sent as WAV files through a standard mix of stems as well as a mix track of the session. All recordings are recorded in Studio One using Audio Technica microphones and the latest software plugins. My drum setup consist of a 6 piece Pacific Drums kit and Zildjian cymbals. Single songs are delivered in 1-2 days. Multiple song projects are delivered in 3-5 days. Genres are not limited, I'm a very flexible and versatile musician that can record any style of music that will suite your project. Extras, such as percussion, are available at your request. Place your order today. I look forward to contributing musically to your next project.
